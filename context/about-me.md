# About Me

Joel is the Operations Manager at The Anchor Group, a real estate team in Palm Coast, FL. His role is social media marketing for the team (Under Contract and SOLD posts, agent success content) plus operations and AI/automation project work. He does not act as a real estate agent and does not handle finances.

Outside of his primary role, Joel is building a wide variety of personal and professional AI projects — including n8n automation workflows, GitHub projects, and an AI integration into the team's Blink account.

## Top pain

The biggest recurring time sink is the social media workflow for Under Contract and SOLD homes. The SOLD process involves multiple rounds of follow-up with agents to gather details and permissions before a post can be drafted, reviewed, and published. An n8n workflow exists but is incomplete. The goal is to reduce the agent touchpoint to a simple checkbox form.

The second biggest drain is the time spent trying to build and connect automations — which is both the work and the obstacle.
