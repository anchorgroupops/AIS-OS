# About The Business

The Anchor Group is a real estate team serving Palm Coast, FL and surrounding areas. The team helps clients buy and sell primarily residential homes and land. Clients are a broad mix of residential home buyers.

The majority of inbound leads come from the Zillow Preferred program — a paid lead-generation partnership with Zillow. This program changes frequently and should be treated as a live knowledge area (check for updates regularly).

Revenue comes from agent commissions on closed transactions. The deal pipeline is tracked in FUB (Follow Up Boss), the team's CRM. Joel does not touch financial operations.

The team uses Blink (joinblink.com) as its group communication platform. Social media (primarily Facebook/Meta) is a key channel for celebrating agent success and staying visible in the local market. Joel owns the social media process end-to-end.
